Future Leaders Program

The Future Leaders Program is an International Coaching Federation (ICF)-approved program that prepares individuals to become future-ready leaders and reinvent themselves as coaches. This program, introduced for the first time in an educational institution, is built upon ICF Core Competencies to develop leadership skills and a forward-thinking approach. Participants are awarded a joint ‘Certificate of Completion’ (ICF & Shoolini University) on completion of the program.

Highlights


  • The only university in India to offer ICF-approved Credit Program

  • Course Credits - 4

  • ICF to award 30 Coach Continuing Education Units (CCEUs)

  • Maiden batch – Summit Research Program (Biotech & Food Tech)


Aligned with NEP

The Future Leaders Program has been designed in accordance with the National Education Policy (NEP) guidelines to provide coaching to support the knowledge, skills, and values of an individual, enabling him to participate fully in a globalised society. The core competencies of ICF adhere to the learning outcomes of NEP — leadership readiness, creativity, communication skills, problem-solving skills, critical thinking, and more.

ICF CCEUS Certification Programs

Continuing Coach Education (CCE) is intended for education organisations and corporates who are interested in having their education and training program accredited by the International Coaching Federation (ICF). CCE is considered supplemental and continuing education for those wishing to acquire new learning, professional development, and/or those who are renewing their ICF Credentials.
