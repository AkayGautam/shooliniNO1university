Our Mission:

The Centre for Leadership Coaching at Shoolini University empowers individuals through inclusive, pro bono coaching, fostering transformative leadership grounded in empathy, compassion, and ICF core competencies. We inspire personal and professional growth, driving lasting positive change and sustainable development in collaboration with industry leaders.

Our Vision:

To cultivate future leaders who are self-aware, resilient, and empowered to significantly impact the world.

What is Coaching?

Coaching is a personalised and collaborative process that helps individuals unlock their potential and achieve their goals. Through coaching, individuals can reflect on their strengths, identify areas for improvement, and develop strategies for unparalleled success.

Why Coaching?

Coaching is essential in today’s dynamic world. It provides participants with the tools and insights needed to navigate challenges and excel in their lives.

What is the Centre for Leadership Coaching?

The Centre for Leadership Coaching at Shoolini University is dedicated to fostering humanistic leaders through its innovative coaching programs. V-Empower, our flagship initiative, offers a range of coaching services designed to support participants in finding clarity and purpose.
