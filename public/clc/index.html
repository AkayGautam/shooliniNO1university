What is the Centre for Leadership Coaching (CLC) Launch Conference?

This two-day premier event is dedicated to Leadership Excellence and Transformative Coaching. This gathering will bring together top industry leaders, renowned coaches, and emerging talent to explore the top innovative practices and strategies in leadership. The conference aligns with ICF core competencies, focusing on empathy and servant leadership, and promises to be a powerful launchpad for the future of leadership and coaching.

Meet the Attendees: Who’s Joining Us

Industry Leaders

Engage with influential leaders such as Magdalena Nowicka Mook, International Coaching Federation CEO, and Prem Singh, NHRD Network President. Gain insights from their experiences and perspectives to drive your professional growth.

Renowned Coaches

Learn from top coaching experts such as Rajesh Ramakrishnan (Advisory Consultant and Executive Coach), Vivek Atray (TEDx Speaker and Ex-IAS), whose insights transform how organisations function and thrive.

Emerging Talent

Network with the brightest future leaders and innovators like Mridu Bhandari (Journalist, Network 18) and dignitaries from defence and academia, who are already making waves in shaping the next generation of industry leadership.
