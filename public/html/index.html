Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who teaches MBA at Shoolini University?

    The School of Business Management boasts faculty from renowned institutions such as the University of Berkeley, IIT, IIM, ISB, and other esteemed universities globally. Industry leaders contribute to the learning experience as visiting faculty, sharing practical insights.

  • Describe the curriculum for MBA at Shoolini University

    Shoolini MBA curriculum is based on inputs from industry, alumni and academia. The curriculum caters to the understanding of the fundamental concepts in business management. Students undergo a compulsory summer internship after the first year.

  • Does Shoolini University provide placements for MBA?

    Yes, Shoolini University guarantees placements for all MBA students. Aligned with 'Mission 130,' the institution aims at 100% placements and 30% placements in top-tier corporate organisations.

  • Where is Shoolini University situated?

    Nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas, Shoolini University's expansive and eco-friendly campus is situated in Solan, Himachal Pradesh, India. It is approximately 73 km from Chandigarh and 67 km from Shimla.

  • Why does Shoolini offer a fully residential MBA?

    The MBA program is 100% residential for 24X7 learning. By spending two years on the campus, students can gain a lifetime of experience. The live-in facility offers intensive learning to MBA students.
